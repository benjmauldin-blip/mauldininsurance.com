What Renters Insurance Covers in Lexington

Three types of protection for Lexington renters — all in one affordable policy.

📱 Your Belongings

Covers your electronics, furniture, clothing, and personal items against fire, theft, vandalism, and certain water damage. Lexington apartment and home renters are often surprised how much they actually own.

⚖️ Your Liability

If a guest is injured in your rental — slips on a wet floor, trips on a rug — you can be sued. Liability coverage pays your legal defense and any settlement, protecting your savings and wages.

🏨 Temporary Housing

If a fire or storm makes your Lexington rental uninhabitable, this pays for a hotel, temporary apartment, and extra living expenses while repairs are made.

🐕 Dog Liability

Most renters policies include dog bite liability coverage. Some breeds are restricted by certain carriers — as an independent agent, we find carriers that cover your specific pet without exclusions.
