What Homeowners Insurance Covers

A standard homeowners policy has several components. We make sure you understand exactly what you have — and what you don't.

🏠 Dwelling Coverage

Covers the structure of your home — walls, roof, floors, built-in appliances — if damaged by fire, wind, hail, lightning, and other covered perils.

📦 Personal Property

Covers your belongings — furniture, electronics, clothing, appliances — if stolen or damaged. We review limits to make sure you're not underinsured.

⚖️ Liability Protection

Covers you if someone is injured on your property or you accidentally damage someone else's property. Includes legal defense costs.

🏨 Additional Living Expenses

Pays for temporary housing and living costs if your home becomes uninhabitable after a covered loss. Critical coverage most people don't think about until they need it.

🌊 Flood Insurance

Standard homeowners policies do NOT cover flooding. If you're in a flood zone — or near Lake Murray — a separate flood policy may be essential.

💎 Scheduled Personal Property

High-value items like jewelry, art, or firearms may need separate scheduling to be fully covered. We make sure your valuables aren't left exposed.
