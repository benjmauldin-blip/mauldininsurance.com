What Lexington Homeowners Need to Know

Coverage needs in Lexington are shaped by local factors most online quoting tools don't account for.

Local Risk Factors

  • Flood risk near Lake Murray and the Saluda River — not covered by standard HO policies
  • Frequent severe thunderstorms with hail and high wind — confirm your wind/hail deductible
  • Many homes built in the 1990s approaching 30-year roof age — carriers are watching
  • Construction costs have risen sharply — your rebuild cost may exceed your current coverage

Coverage Checklist

  • Dwelling coverage at current rebuild cost — not market value
  • Replacement Cost Value on personal property — not Actual Cash Value
  • Liability at $300,000 minimum — the default $100,000 is rarely enough
  • Loss of use coverage adequate for 90+ days of temporary housing
  • Flood insurance reviewed and in place if near water
