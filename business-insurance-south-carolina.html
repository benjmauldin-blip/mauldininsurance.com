Business Coverage We Offer

From sole proprietors to growing small businesses, we find the right coverage combination for your specific operation.

⚖️ General Liability

Covers bodily injury and property damage claims made against your business. Often required by clients and landlords. The foundation of any business insurance plan.

🏢 Business Owner's Policy (BOP)

Bundles general liability and commercial property coverage into one convenient policy — typically at a lower cost than buying each separately. Ideal for most small businesses.

🚐 Commercial Auto

Personal auto insurance doesn't cover vehicles used for business purposes. If you or your employees drive for work, commercial auto coverage is essential.

👷 Workers' Compensation

Required in South Carolina for most businesses with four or more employees. Covers medical costs and lost wages for employees injured on the job.

💻 Professional Liability

Also called Errors & Omissions (E&O). Protects you if a client claims your professional advice or services caused them financial harm.

🔒 Cyber Liability

Covers costs related to data breaches, cyberattacks, and privacy violations. Increasingly important for any business that stores customer data.
