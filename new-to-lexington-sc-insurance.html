What's Different About Insurance in SC

South Carolina has specific rules and risks that affect your coverage needs.

⛈️

Separate Wind & Hail Deductibles

Many SC home policies carry a separate, higher deductible for wind and hail damage — sometimes 1–2% of your home's insured value. Make sure you understand yours before storm season.

🌊

Flood Is Not Covered by Default

Unlike some northern states where flooding is rare, Lexington County has real flood risk. Your standard HO policy covers zero flood damage — a separate NFIP or private flood policy is essential for many addresses.

🚗

SC Auto Minimums Are Low

SC requires just 25/50/25 liability coverage — far too little for most families. We recommend at least 100/300/100 to protect your assets, especially as a new homeowner.

📋

Credit Scores Affect Your Rate

SC insurers are permitted to use credit-based insurance scores to set rates. Knowing this upfront helps us shop carriers who will treat your profile most favorably.
